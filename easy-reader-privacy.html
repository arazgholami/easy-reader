Easy Reader Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Easy Reader ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Easy Reader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect any personal information about you
  • Track your browsing history or reading habits
  • Use any analytics or tracking technologies
  • Store any information about the content you read
  • Share any data with third parties

3. Data Storage

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage to save your preferences, including:

  • Text size and font preferences
  • Color scheme settings
  • Instant mode preference
  • Other display preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

4. Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Required to access and modify the current tab's content to display the reading overlay and apply text direction detection.
  • contextMenus: Used to provide a right-click context menu option for quickly activating the reader view on selected text.
  • storage: Used to save your preferences between browsing sessions.

5. Remote Code

The Extension includes the Vazir font file (vazir.woff2) for proper RTL text rendering. This font is loaded from the extension's package and does not make external network requests.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
